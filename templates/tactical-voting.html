This page aggregates constituency-level recommendations for tactical voting in the 2024 British general election.

A number of websites make recommendations about how to vote tactically in a constituency in order to elect a non-Conservative MP. These sites don't always agree! This page shows recommendations, and indicates where there is disagreement.

You can click "Hide Boring" to show only the seats where (a) there is disagreement about which party has the best chance of keeping the Tories out, or (b) the recommended party differs from the party that currently holds the seat.

An empty cell means that the website makes no recommendation in a particular constitency. A question mark indicates that the website isn't yet sure what to recommend.
