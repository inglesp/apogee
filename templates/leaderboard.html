This page attempts to evaluate each model's forecast, by calculating the root mean square error between each forecast's prediction of each party's share of the vote in each seat, and the results, as they come in.

I think this is a better measure of accuracy than simply counting the number of seats that were called correctly, because it should reward the models that best represent reality.

Health warning: this may not be the best way of evaluating a forecast! Please let me know if you think I've made a mistake.
